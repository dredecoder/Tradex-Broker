FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you start investing with TradeEx.

How do I deposit funds into TradeEx?
TradeEx accepts deposits via Apple Gift Cards only. Simply purchase a gift card for the amount of your chosen plan, scratch the card to reveal the code, upload a photo of the card, and enter the card number in the deposit form. Our team will verify and credit your wallet within minutes.
How long does it take to receive my returns?
All investment plans run for 7 days. Once your plan matures, your payout is automatically processed and credited to your TradeEx wallet — typically within 1 minute to 1 hour after the 7-day period ends.
Can I invest in multiple plans at the same time?
Yes! You can hold multiple active investment plans simultaneously. There is no limit on the number of plans you can invest in at the same time.
How do I withdraw my earnings?
Once your returns are in your wallet, you can submit a withdrawal request via Bitcoin, USDT, or bank transfer. Our team reviews and processes all withdrawals within 24 hours. The minimum withdrawal amount is $20.
Is my investment safe with TradeEx?
TradeEx employs SSL encryption, secure authentication, and strict data protection practices. All deposits are manually verified by our team before funds are credited, ensuring the highest level of security for every transaction.
What denominations of Apple Gift Cards are accepted?
We accept any Apple Gift Card denomination that matches your chosen plan amount. You may combine multiple cards to reach the required deposit amount for your selected investment tier.
